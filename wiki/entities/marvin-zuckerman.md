# Marvin Zuckerman

University of Delaware psychologist who developed the concept and measurement of [sensation seeking](../concepts/sensation-seeking.md) as a personality trait. His Sensation Seeking Scale (SSS, Form V, 1978) remains the standard instrument.

## Key Contributions

- Defined sensation seeking as pursuit of varied, novel, complex, intense experiences with willingness to take risks
- Identified four dimensions: Thrill and Adventure Seeking, Experience Seeking, Disinhibition, Boredom Susceptibility
- Established biological basis: low MAO-B activity, dopaminergic system differences, cortical augmenting responses
- Built on Hebb's optimal arousal theory to explain individual differences in stimulation needs

## Major Works

- *Sensation Seeking: Beyond the Optimal Level of Arousal* (1979)
- *Behavioral Expressions and Biosocial Bases of Sensation Seeking* (1994)
