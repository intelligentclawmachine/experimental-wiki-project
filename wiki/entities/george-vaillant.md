# George Vaillant

Psychiatrist who directed the [[harvard-study-of-adult-development|Harvard Study of Adult Development]] for over 30 years. Author of *Triumphs of Experience* (2012) and *Aging Well* (2002).

## Key Contributions

- **Five lessons for a meaningful life**: loving relationships, money/power are secondary, happiness can increase over time, connection matters more with age, mature coping trumps narcissism
- **Six factors for healthy aging**: physical activity, no alcohol abuse/smoking, [[mature-coping-mechanisms|mature coping]], healthy weight, stable marriage, education
- **Hierarchy of emotional adaptations**: immature (denial, projection) vs. mature (altruism, anticipation, suppression, sublimation, humor)
- Famous quote: "One is love. The other is finding a way of coping with life that does not push love away."
