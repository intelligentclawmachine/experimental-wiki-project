# Erin Westgate

University of Florida psychologist. Co-developer (with [[shigehiro-oishi|Shigehiro Oishi]]) of the [[psychological-richness|psychological richness]] framework.

## Key Contributions

- Introduced "psychologically rich life" as a third dimension of well-being (2022), beyond happiness and meaning
- Demonstrated cross-cultural validity of the concept
- Key insight: "Interesting experiences aren't always pleasant experiences" — some people prioritize depth and perspective change over comfort or purpose
