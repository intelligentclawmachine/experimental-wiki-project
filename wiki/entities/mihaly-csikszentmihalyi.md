# Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi

Hungarian-American psychologist (1934–2021) who pioneered the scientific study of [flow](../concepts/flow-state.md) and optimal experience. Co-founder of positive psychology alongside Martin Seligman.

## Key Contributions

- Defined **flow** as a state of complete absorption where action and awareness merge
- Identified eight characteristics of flow and the challenge-skill balance as the core mechanism
- Developed the Experience Sampling Method (ESM) for studying subjective experience in real time
- Described the **autotelic personality** — individuals predisposed to experiencing flow across activities
- Demonstrated that flow correlates with life satisfaction, meaning, and well-being

## Major Works

- *Flow: The Psychology of Optimal Experience* (1990)
- *Creativity: Flow and the Psychology of Discovery and Invention* (1996)
- *Finding Flow: The Psychology of Engagement with Everyday Life* (1997)
