# Amy Edmondson

Harvard Business School professor who introduced the concept of "team psychological safety" and demonstrated its link to learning behavior and team performance.

## Key Contributions

- **Psychological safety construct** — "a shared belief that a team is safe for interpersonal risk-taking"
- **Error-reporting paradox** — the best hospital teams reported more errors because they felt safe doing so
- **Safety vs. trust distinction** — psychological safety is a group climate; trust is a dyadic relationship
- **The Fearless Organization** (2018) — book applying the research to organizational design

## Core Insight

Performance in learning environments depends not on eliminating errors but on creating conditions where errors are surfaced, discussed, and learned from. Psychological safety is the prerequisite for organizational learning.
