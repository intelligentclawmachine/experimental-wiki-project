# Laura King

Curators' Distinguished Professor of Psychological Sciences at the University of Missouri. Senior researcher on the [[hope|hope]]-meaning study.

## Key Contributions

- Led the research team that identified hope as the strongest positive emotion predictor of meaning in life (published in *Emotion*, 2025)
- Noted that meaning in life predicts happiness, better relationships, improved physical health, and higher income
- Collaborated with Peking University researchers on cross-cultural validation
