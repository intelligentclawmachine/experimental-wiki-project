# Shigehiro Oishi

University of Chicago psychologist. Co-developer (with [[erin-westgate|Erin Westgate]]) of the [[psychological-richness|psychological richness]] framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced the three-dimensional model of well-being: happiness, meaning, and psychological richness
- Scale development began in 2015; term formally introduced in 2022
- Emphasizes that the "good life" question has more than two answers
