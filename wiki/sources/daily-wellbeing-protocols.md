# Daily Well-Being Protocols

A compendium of 15 evidence-based daily behaviors for intraday well-being, drawn from multiple research sources including Huberman Lab protocols, Stanford RCTs, PMC meta-analyses, and longitudinal studies.

## The Protocols at a Glance

| # | Practice | Minimum Dose | Best Evidence For |
|---|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| 1 | Morning sunlight | 5-10 min (bright), 15-20 min (cloudy) | Circadian alignment, sleep quality |
| 2 | Caffeine delay | 90-120 min after waking | Sustained energy, no afternoon crash |
| 3 | Exercise | 10 min any movement | Mood, depression, cognitive function |
| 4 | Cold exposure | 1-5 min, 2-4x/week | Dopamine (+250%), alertness, resilience |
| 5 | Breathwork (cyclic sighing) | 5 min | Mood (+1.89 affect), anxiety (-3.85) |
| 6 | Meditation | 10 min | Attention, working memory, stress |
| 7 | Nature exposure | 10-20 min | Cortisol (-21%), mental health |
| 8 | Gratitude journaling | 3-5 min (3 good things) | Happiness, depression reduction (6mo+) |
| 9 | Sleep consistency | 7-9 hrs, same time daily | Depression, anxiety, metabolic health |
| 10 | Hydration | Throughout day | Cognition, mood, energy |
| 11 | Protein-forward breakfast | 25-40g protein first meal | Blood sugar stability, sustained energy |
| 12 | Micro social interactions | Throughout day | Life satisfaction, belonging |
| 13 | Digital boundaries | Phone-free morning/meals/bedtime | Depression reduction, presence |
| 14 | Self-compassion touch | 20 seconds | Stress, anxiety, depression reduction |
| 15 | Walking | 4,000-10,000 steps | Mood, dementia risk, mortality |
