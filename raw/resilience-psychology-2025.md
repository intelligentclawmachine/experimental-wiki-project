# Resilience (Research Synthesis, 2025)

## Definitions
Masten: "ordinary magic" — common capacity from basic adaptational systems. Bounce-back (return to baseline) vs. bounce-forward (new growth beyond prior level).

## Kauai Study (Werner & Smith)
~700 children from 1955. Among high-adversity group, 1/3 thrived. Protective: stable caring relationship, internal locus of control, self-regulatory capacity.

## Four Trajectories (Bonanno)
After adversity: resilience (stable functioning), recovery (initial distress → gradual return), delayed reactions, chronic dysfunction. Resilience is most common trajectory.

## Psychological Capital (Luthans)
PsyCap: self-efficacy + optimism + hope + resilience. Predicts workplace performance and well-being.

## Grit (Duckworth)
Passion + perseverance for long-term goals. Overlaps with but distinct from resilience (sustained effort vs. adversity response).

## Resilience vs. PTG (Tedeschi)
Resilience = maintaining/regaining functioning. PTG = qualitative transformation. Surviving vs. being transformed.

## Building Resilience
Social support, cognitive flexibility (reappraisal), physical health. Most reliable contributors.
