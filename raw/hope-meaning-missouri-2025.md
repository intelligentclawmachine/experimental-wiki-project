# Hope is the Key to a Meaningful Life

Source: University of Missouri
URL: https://showme.missouri.edu/2025/hope-is-the-key-to-a-meaningful-life-according-to-new-research/
Published in: *Emotion* (journal)

## Study Overview
University of Missouri researchers conducted six studies involving more than 2,300 participants from diverse backgrounds to examine how hope relates to life meaning.

## Key Findings
Hope stands apart as one of the strongest positive emotions directly fostering a sense of meaning. The team analyzed multiple emotions including amusement, contentment, excitement, and happiness. "Only hope consistently predicted a stronger sense of meaning."

## Research Team
- **Megan Edwards** — Earned doctorate at Mizzou; now postdoctoral scholar at Duke University
- **Laura King** — Curators' Distinguished Professor of Psychological Sciences at Mizzou
- **Co-authors:** Jordan A. Booker and Kevin Cook (Mizzou); Miao Miao and Yiqun Gan (Peking University, China)

## Significance
Laura King noted that experiencing meaning in life predicts important outcomes: happiness, better quality relationships, improved physical health, and higher income.

## Practical Strategies to Build Hope
- Appreciating positive moments, even small ones
- Seizing opportunities during uncertain times
- Recognizing growth and potential
- Engaging in nurturing activities
- Remembering that situations can change
